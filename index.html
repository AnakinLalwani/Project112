Teachable Machine(My one)
VS
Mobilenet (Pre-made one)

Aim: To find the differences between my AI and Mobilenet

Observation:
1) In the one which was made by me in teachable machine has more of a chance at failing as there was only a limited number of pictures put in and most objects are different
2)Mobilenet works most of the time as it has been trained with multiple images but sometimes goes wrong(Click the image to see both)



Conclusion:
1) Teachable Machine is more likely to identify objects that have been trained into it
2) Mobilenet can identify most images as it has been trained with images from the internet but is most likely to fail if the object is very colorful and different from all the other objects in its category
